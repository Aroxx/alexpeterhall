About Me:

I have been building websites in some form another since I was a kid and people had free Angelfire/Geocities websites and dial-up internet. Growing up in the dotcom boom I developed a great interest for technology and decided to go to school for Computer Information Systems. I have continued to build websites utilizing the latest web technologies and I enjoy keeping up with current tech trends. I like to work with open-source content management systems to build websites that clients can easily manage and update independently.

I currently work as a team lead in a production support roll supporting an Ecommerce website with well over 1 million unique visitors per day. Our team is responsible for triaging, troubleshooting, and resolving any and all production incidents that occur on the website. We deal with anything from content and UI issues to infrastructure outages and everything in between. We work closely with development, UX, content, operations, and various other teams to drive all incidents to resolution. It is a very fast paced work environment with high visibility to senior leadership and executives that keeps me on my toes every day.

On a more personal level, I am married to an incredible woman named Jane and we live together with our two cats Tula (fatty) and Lily (monster). We are both very active and focused on health, physical fitness, and eating organic and sustainable foods. We do a lot of running, strength training, cycling, and various other physical training. I like to participate in events and activities that include road races, obstacle course races, GORUCK, paintball, disc golf, and playing my violin and guitar.
